Welcomes/Farewells

Two of Cinnamon's most popular features are the fully customisable welcome and farewell messages. The messages are sent to whichever text channel you've set as the message channel and can include mentions of channels, users, roles, and even the user that joined or left! Not only are the welcome and farewell messages fully customisable, you can also have multiple welcome or farewell messages per guild if you'd like one to be picked at random when a user joins or leaves.

Multiplayer Games

The entire reason for Cinnamon's creation was because I wanted a bot that had several multiplayer games which I could play with guild members and friends whenever I got bored. At the moment games are restricted by channel but I intend to implement cross-guild gameplay in the near future. Some games include Cards Against Humanity, Connect Four, a game of my own creation named Battle, and soon Tetris!

Fun and Miscellaneous

Cinnamon has dozens of fun commands to keep any guild entertained, ranging from ships and waifu ratings to gaydars and an intentionally silly insult generator. Aside from that there are also several utility commands including a quotation system and avatar enlarger guaranteed to display the largest possible version of a user's profile picture.

Stats and Integrations

Another popular feature is the guild, channel, and user based activity tracking system which includes all kinds of stats including average hourly activity and records such as most messages sent in a day. Soon Cinnamon will even be able to analyse these statistics to determine the health of a guild and potential remedial action to keep the guild lively if necessary. Cinnamon can also retrieve user data thanks to its integrations with League of Legends, Fortnite, Osu and Overwatch. The aim is to eventually make it possible to link game accounts to Cinnamon so that user data is synced with Discord, but this may not be implemented for a while if at all.
