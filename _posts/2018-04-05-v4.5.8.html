  • Cards Against Humanity

    This is a game I was really excited to try to recreate, it's always tons of fun to play while in a vc with some friends. The basis of the game is that everyone starts with 7 white cards and a black card is placed in the centre. The black cards each have a sentence with gaps in it or a question of some kind, while the white cards contain a phrase designed to fill in the gaps of the black cards. Each turn a new black card is placed in the middle and players choose cards from their hands to create the funniest sentence when combined with the black card. The winner is chosen by a Card Czar and the first player to 10 points wins.
    In my adaptation of this game I've included a lot of different configurable options:
    • The number of blank cards (allow players to enter their own answers) in the deck (default: 10)
    • Number of seconds before skipping a player's turn for idling (default: 60)
    • The score needed to win (default: 10)
    • Maximum number of players (default: 10)
    • Toggle whether users can join the game while it's ongoing or not
    If you encounter any bugs please let me know. For more information and command usage see the commands page.
